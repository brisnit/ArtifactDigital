Reimagining retail with a global electronics brand.

We partnered with a leading electronics brand to build an immersive AR showroom — bringing premium appliances to life through interactive in-store technology that drives engagement and informed purchases.

The experience

A showroom you could scan into.

A QR-powered attractor loop opened the AR journey straight on the customer's phone — no app, no store account, no waiting. Object detection recognized each appliance on the floor and unlocked interactive overlays: feature highlights, 3D animations, and lifestyle context tuned to the product in front of them.

The roll-out covered select premium appliance stores, fully integrated with the brand's high-end aesthetic. Visually striking codes invited interaction, while intuitive, app-free navigation made access effortless — sparking curiosity and deeper engagement right at the point of decision.
