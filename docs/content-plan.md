# Artifact Digital — 50-Article Content Plan

**Purpose:** build topical authority in the areas we sell, so Google ranks us and LLMs cite us.
This is the one growth lever that needs nobody's permission.

## How this works

**Clusters, not one-offs.** Search engines and LLMs reward *depth in a topic*, not scattered
posts. Each cluster maps to a service page. Every article links up to its service page and
sideways to siblings. That web of links is what signals "these people own this subject."

**The rules:**
- Every article links to **its service page** (the money page) and **2–3 sibling articles**.
- Write for one **primary keyword** but answer the question fully — GEO rewards extractable,
  quotable passages, not keyword stuffing.
- Use a **question-shaped H2** wherever real people ask a real question. That's what gets
  lifted into AI answers and featured snippets.
- 2,000–3,500 words. Opinionated. First person. No agency filler.
- Meta title ≤ 60 chars, meta description ≤ 155.
- **Publish one a week.** Consistency beats bursts. 50 weeks ≈ topical authority.

**Intent legend:** `INFO` = learning (top of funnel) · `COMM` = evaluating (bottom of funnel,
these convert) · `NAV` = brand/opinion.
